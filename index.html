Design 360

24hr Designathon | Esya 2018

Design 360 is a designathon where the teams give solutions to critical problems through design, prototyping, and testing ideas. During the event, the students will be required to submit their idea and an interface/video interface/video, followed by a presentation.

Rules


Eligibility criteria All (school/college) students are eligible.
Team Size 3-5 members (Cross college/school teams allowed)
Solo Participation Lone wolves are allowed to make teams on the spot
Event Duration 16 hours
Entry Bring your school/college ID for entry.
Resources Bring your own laptops, chargers, wires, cameras, extension cords, etc. We will provide wifi, markers, charts, etc.
Plaigarism Plagiarism of any kind will not be accepted and would result in disqualification.
Belongings Your belongings are your responsibilty. The D360 team shall not be held responsible for the loss of your items.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can come?

All school and college students are allowed. Just make sure that you get a valid student ID.

How much does it cost?

Design 360 costs nothing. Registering and attending is free, and you get free food, prizes, swag and a great learning experience.

What should I bring?

Bring your valid student ID, laptop, phones, charger, wires, camera, etc. (we will be providing internet, mattresses, food and coffee).

When and where is it?

Our event will be held on August 17th to 18th as a 16 hour overnight event. It will take place inside IIIT Delhi in our Library building.

Can I have an example problem statement?

Previous problem statements can be viewed here. Projects created by our participants last time can be found here.

How do I form a team?

You'll have time to find a team when you arrive. If you already have a team, just list your teammates when you register.

What about accomodation?

We will make special arrangements in case you arrive a day before the event. Just shoot an email to one of the organizers.

What can I win?

We're working with some awesome companies to give out tons of prizes.
